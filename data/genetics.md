# Genetics

> ⚠️ READ THIS FIRST. Genetics is the most sensitive data here.
>
> Your genome is permanent, it implicates blood relatives, and it can carry insurance/employment/stigma risk. Think hard before putting raw variants into a cloud AI.
>
> Safer options:
> - Keep this file **local only** (don't sync it to a cloud AI), or
> - Store only **categories** here ("I have a carrier variant relevant to family planning") rather than specific genes/variants, or
> - Leave it out entirely until you've decided.
>
> If you delete this file, that's a fine default.

## What I'm comfortable noting (example, category-level)
- Whole-genome sequenced via [provider], [year].
- Actionable finding I now screen for with my doctor: yes / no.
- Carrier status relevant to family planning: noted with my doctor.
- Pharmacogenomics: not yet tested.

_Do not paste raw variant tables here unless you've accepted the risk above._
