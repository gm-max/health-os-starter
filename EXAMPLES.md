## 3. Coach: "should I add a daily multivitamin?"

> Short answer: skip the multivitamin, fix the two things that are actually low.
>
> Your bloodwork shows two real gaps, vitamin D (22) and ferritin (18), and you're already dosing both directly (protocol/supplements.md). A multivitamin would add small amounts of a dozen things you don't need and a low dose of iron that could interfere with how you time the targeted one. Everything else in your panel is in range (data/bloodwork.md).
>
> What would change my answer: a future panel showing a new deficiency that's easier to cover broadly (e.g. several B vitamins low at once). Until then, targeted beats broad.
>
> Not medical advice; confirm supplement changes with your doctor, especially iron.
